Using

This app is used to simulate fog of war in tabletop games. It allows one side to setup and move their units without the other side seeing what is going on. When one side's turn ends, the app is handed to the opposing side which in turn moves units hidden from the other side. When units move next to each other, they are revealed by the app and must be placed on the tabletop at the according sectors. Then, your favorite tabletop rules must be used to handle onboard action. After a full turn of tabletop rules, both sides continue moving hidden units to support their revealed units or engage elsewhere.

Click on a unit to select it, then click on the unit's new position. When all units are moved, hit End movement and hand over this app to the other side.
